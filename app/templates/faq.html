Frequently Asked Questions

A Surebet (or arbitrage bet) is a set of bets placed across different bookmakers that guarantees a profit regardless of the event outcome. Our app scans odds from multiple sources and identifies these opportunities automatically.
A Value Bet occurs when a bookmaker's odds underestimate the true probability of an event happening. Over time, betting only on value outcomes yields positive expected value (EV). Our system compares bookmaker odds with sharp or market-efficient lines to detect these.
A Middle is when you bet on opposite lines (e.g., -3.5 and +2.0) at different bookmakers and have a range of outcomes where both bets win. This allows you to “hit the middle” for higher profit while minimizing risk.
The app pulls real-time odds from multiple APIs (e.g., TheOddsAPI) across regions and markets such as H2H, spreads, and totals. Data is cleaned, normalized, and compared to find pricing inefficiencies.
Profit is based on the total stake and odds distribution. For surebets, stakes are balanced across outcomes to lock in guaranteed returns. For valuebets, expected profit is determined by comparing implied probability with true probability. For middles, profit depends on whether both, one, or neither bets land.
